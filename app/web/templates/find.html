Other options worth knowing about

The list above shows schools your address is zoned for — guaranteed-seat schools. Your child likely has more options:

  • Charter schools — citywide, lottery-based admission. Not tied to your address.
  • Gifted & Talented (G&T) programs — citywide, test-based admission for K-5.
  • Magnet schools and choice districts — District 1 (Lower East Side) and District 7 (South Bronx) are entirely unzoned for elementary; Districts 2, 3, 15 use choice-based admissions for middle school.
  • Pre-K and 3-K — separate citywide enrollment, not tied to elementary zoning.

For older students on alternative paths (not relevant if you’re searching for an elementary or middle school):

  • Transfer high schools — admit by interview/referral; serve over-age, under-credited HS students.
  • District 79 (Alternative Schools and Programs) — GED/HSE prep, Pathways to Graduation (P2G), programs at Rikers and youth facilities, LYFE for student parents.

The official application portal is MySchools; directory and admissions detail are at schools.nyc.gov.
